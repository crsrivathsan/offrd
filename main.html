Help & Setup Guide
Get Offrd for your next role in minutes
Getting your API keys
1

AI Engine Configuration

Offrd is fully configured and ready to use.

Step 1 — Go to console.anthropic.com and sign up or log in.

Step 2 — In the left sidebar, click API Keys.

Step 3 — Click Create Key, give it a name (e.g. "Offrd"), then copy it immediately.

Your key looks like: sk-ant-api03-...

⚠ The key is shown only once. Copy it before closing the dialog. If you miss it, delete the key and create a new one.
💡 Add a payment method under Billing to activate the key. New accounts get free credits. Each job search costs approximately $0.01–$0.03.
2

Get your JSearch API Key — OpenWeb Ninja (free)

JSearch by OpenWeb Ninja fetches real-time job listings from Google Jobs — covering LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Naukri and more.

Step 1 — Go to openwebninja.com/api/jsearch and click Try for Free.

Step 2 — Sign up for a free account (no credit card required).

Step 3 — In your dashboard, go to Active APIs → JSearch → copy your x-api-key.

Your key looks like: abc123de-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx

💡 Free tier: 200 requests/month, no credit card needed. Each search uses 4 requests (one per board). That's ~50 full searches per month — plenty for active job hunting. Quota resets monthly.
⚠ Monitor your usage from your Offrd account dashboard. If you hit your search limit, it resets monthly or you can upgrade your plan.
3

Enter your keys in Settings

Click ⚙ Settings in the top-right navigation. Paste both keys and click Save Keys. Keys are stored only in your browser's localStorage and never sent to any third party.

💡 The status indicator in the nav bar turns green when both keys are configured.
How to use the app
4

Upload your resume

On the Upload Resume step, drag and drop your PDF or DOCX resume onto the upload zone — or paste the text directly. Our AI extracts your name, skills, experience level, and suggests job search keywords automatically.

5

Configure your job search

Refine the pre-filled keywords, select your target country, set experience level and job type. Toggle which job boards to search and adjust the ranking priority sliders to weight what matters most to you.

6

Review matches & take action

Jobs are ranked with a 0–100 match score broken down by Relevancy, Seniority Fit, Low Competition, and Salary Match. Click any job to see the skill gap chart and salary context. Use ✦ Tailor Resume to get a AI-rewritten resume for that specific role, downloadable as PDF or DOCX. Save interesting jobs to your Pipeline board to track applications.
