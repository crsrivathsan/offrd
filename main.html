Offrd

An AI-powered job search assistant that scrapes live listings across the world's top job boards, scores them against your resume, and helps you apply with a tailored, ATS-optimised resume — all in one place.

● Live Job Scraping ✦ AI Resume Tailoring 📊 Match Scoring
📄
Resume Intelligence

Claude reads your resume and extracts skills, experience level, and keywords to personalise every search.

🔍
Multi-Board Scraping

Fetches real-time listings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Naukri and more via JSearch — Google Jobs aggregated.

🎯
Smart Match Scoring

Every job is scored across relevancy, seniority fit, applicant competition, and salary match — weighted by your priorities.

✦
Tailored Resume Export

Get your resume rewritten for any job and download it instantly as a formatted PDF or DOCX file.

📋
Application Pipeline

Track every application from Saved through to Offer with a Kanban-style board, fully persisted in your browser.

🕐
Search History

Re-run any of your last 5 searches in one click, with all parameters restored exactly as before.
