How Offrd came to be
The origin story is less "garage startup" and more "one too many tabs open at 1am." Picture six browser tabs, three different job boards, a resume that needed a different version for every single application, and the slow realisation that "tailoring your resume" mostly means copy-pasting the same bullet points and hoping nobody notices. After the fortieth tab, a simple thought emerged: what if one tool just did all of this — and didn't charge for the privilege, spam your phone, or sell your data to the highest bidder? Offrd was the answer to that very tired, very caffeinated question.
How to use it
Upload or paste your resume, then let Offrd analyse it and search live job boards on your behalf. Review AI-scored matches, tailor your resume for any role in one click, and track every application through the pipeline — from saved to offer.
