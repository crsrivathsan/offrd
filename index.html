Searches LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and Naukri simultaneously in real time via live job boards — no stale listings, no duplicates, always the freshest opportunities available.

HOW IT WORKS

From resume to offer
in four steps

The entire process takes under five minutes to set up. AI handles the heavy lifting.

1

Upload your resume

Drag and drop your PDF or paste your resume text. Our AI instantly extracts your name, skills, experience level, and career goals — and pre-fills your search settings automatically.

2

Configure your search

Refine your job title keywords, select your target country, set experience level and job type, choose which boards to search, and adjust the AI ranking weights to match what matters most to you.

3

Review AI-ranked matches

Offrd fetches live listings and scores every job against your profile. See exactly why each role is a strong or weak match, view the full job description, and compare salary benchmarks — all before you apply.

4

Apply with a tailored resume

For every role you want to pursue, our AI rewrites your resume to match the specific job description. Download as PDF or DOCX and apply directly. Track your progress on the Kanban pipeline board.
