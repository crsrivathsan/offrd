Help & Setup Guide

Getting started
1

Offrd is ready to use

No setup needed — sign in and start searching. Offrd handles everything behind the scenes, including job search and AI matching.

How to use the app
2

Upload your resume

Drag and drop your PDF or DOCX resume onto the upload zone — or paste the text directly. Our AI extracts your name, skills, experience level, and suggests job search keywords automatically.

3

Configure your job search

Refine the pre-filled keywords, select your target country, set experience level and job type. Toggle which job boards to search and adjust the ranking priority sliders to weight what matters most to you.

4

Review matches & take action

Jobs are ranked with a 0–100 match score broken down by Relevancy, Seniority Fit, Low Competition, and Salary Match. Click any job to see the skill gap chart and salary context. Use ✦ Tailor Resume to get an AI-rewritten resume, or ✉ Cover Letter for a tailored cover letter. Save interesting jobs to your Pipeline board to track applications.
